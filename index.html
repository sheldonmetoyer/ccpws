Products

Corpus Christi Polymers is one of the largest manufacturers of PET and PTA in the world. Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) and Purified Terephthalic Acid (PTA) are essential materials in the production of high-quality plastics and fibers. PET is widely used in packaging, particularly for food and beverages, due to its strength, lightweight, and recyclability. PTA is a key raw material in manufacturing PET and polyester, ensuring the production of durable, versatile products. Constantly seeking the most advanced and efficient processes, the materials that we manufacture bring us a wide range of essential and practical products that are able to meet the demands of various industries, supporting sustainability and innovation in every application.

PTA

Purified Terephthalic Acid (PTA) is an organic compound that appears as a white crystalline powder and is primarily used as a precursor in the production of PET. PTA is produced through the oxidation of paraxylene in the presence of a catalyst, playing a crucial role in the polymerization process that creates PET. PTA is a versatile and efficient component, essential to numerous industries, and contributes to the production of high-quality, durable, and sustainable materials

PET

Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) is a plastic polymer used to manufacture packaging applications for the beverage, food, and personal care industries, with key technological advantages in its versatility, durability, heat resistance, lightweight, cost-competitiveness and 100% recyclability.

Desal

At Corpus Christi Polymers, our commitment to sustainable and responsible manufacturing is exemplified by our state-of-the-art desalination plant located in Corpus Christi, Texas. This advanced facility converts seawater into high-quality industrial water, providing a reliable and sustainable water source for our production processes. This significant infrastructure investment exemplifies our dedication to environmental stewardship and efficient resource management.
